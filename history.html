Our town traces its history back to the early days of lumbering in Michigan!


  • The reason we’ve chosen this theme is because it represents our town ‘Majorvill’. In the 1870s and 1880s, Majorville was a booming lumber town!

    Following the U. S. Civil War, the veterans returned home, and John Major settled in Northern Michigan. In late 1854, purchased the 122 acres from Orange and Sally Risdon of Saline, MI.
